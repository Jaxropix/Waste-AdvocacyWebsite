Why Southeast Asia?

Due to Southeast Asia's demand for recycleable materials, companies and countries have exploit this as a loophole to send their trash to multiple countries in the region. The region have less limitations on regulations of waste import and exports. The companies also can disguise their shipments of waste as shipments of goods. This has negatively impacted the region's envionment, residents' health, and etc.
